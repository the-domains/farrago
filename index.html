Restaurant Reviews

I am going to do some quick reviews of some of the places our dining group has visited and enjoyed. If I get a chance, I shall add pictures later.

Nonna’s

One of the first places we visited and loved was Nonna's (4115 Lomo Alto Dr.) Great Italian food. Except for a few standards, their menu changes regularly. Their pasta and pizza are exceptional. When the group went a year or so ago, I had Iberico Lardo on a crostini and a raviolo with an egg on top. Absolutely perfect.

Gemma

Our next venture was to Gemma (2323 North Henderson, Ste. 109) One thing I love about our group is that most of us will try about anything. On our trip to Gemma, I had Crispy Veal Sweetbreads with whole grain mustard for an appetizer and braised Veal cheeks for the entree. Definitely a place to return to.
